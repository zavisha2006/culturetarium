Hello! My name is Zavish. Welcome to Culturetarium! In today's society, there is a large amount of disconnect between people around the world, and people with different backgrounds. This problem has improved in the past few decades, but it's still a prevalent problem in society. Learning about cultures around the world can help introduce people to different peoples' ways of living, can help end the disconnect in society, and is an important step to creating peace in the world as well as being able to live among one another in harmony.

This site was created to help fix this problem -- to teach others about culture in order to foster a greater appreciation and understanding of cultures around the world that may have a different way of living than people are used to. To learn about a certain cultural aspect, simply click randomize, and you will see a short description of a cultural aspect followed by a link to read more about the topic. You can submit an idea of a cultural topic by contacting me from the contact section of this website.

Let's learn cultures!
